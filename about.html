About Us

Overview

PurrfectMatch is a dedicated non-profit organization founded on the belief that every kitten deserves a life defined by safety, warmth, and affection. Established as a sanctuary for stray and struggling felines, the foundation focuses on rescuing those who have been abandoned or born into harsh conditions. By providing immediate medical attention, nutritional support, and a stable environment, PurrfectMatch transforms the lives of vulnerable kittens, ensuring they are no longer left to navigate the world alone and unprotected.

Our History

From a single stray in 2020 to a nationwide community, PurrfectMatch has come a long way. We are thrilled to announce that we have now secured loving homes for over 50 cats across Nepal, bridging the gap between vulnerable kittens and their forever families. This milestone is a testament to the power of compassion. We owe a huge thank you to every supporter and volunteer who believed in our mission—together, we’re ensuring that no kitten has to face the world alone.
